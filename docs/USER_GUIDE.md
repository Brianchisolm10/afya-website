## Navigating the Website

### Main Navigation

The AFYA website features a clean, simple navigation menu with seven main sections:

**Home** - Return to the homepage to see featured programs, shop items, and community impact

**Programs** - Browse all 7 AFYA wellness programs and choose the right path for you

**Shop** - Purchase AFYA merchandise to support the organization (25% of every purchase goes to community programs)

**Impact** - Learn about and participate in community initiatives including donations, sponsorships, and gear drives

**Start** - Begin your personalized intake form to receive custom wellness packets

**Login** - Access your account dashboard to view packets, track progress, and manage your profile

### Mobile Navigation

On mobile devices, tap the menu icon (☰) in the top right to access the navigation menu. The menu slides in from the side for easy access to all sections.

### Footer Navigation

The footer provides quick access to all pages organized by category:

- **Company**: About, Mission, Team, Careers
- **Programs**: All 7 program types
- **Shop**: Apparel, Accessories, Drops, Collections
- **Impact**: Donate, Sponsor, Gear Drive, Equipment (Coming Soon), Foundations
- **Support**: FAQ, Contact, Help, Policies, Privacy, Terms, Refunds

## Getting Started

### What is the Intake Process?

The intake process is a personalized questionnaire that helps us understand your goals, preferences, and needs. Based on your answers, we'll create customized nutrition plans, workout programs, or specialized guidance just for you.

### How Long Does It Take?

The time varies depending on which path you choose:

- **Nutrition Only**: 10-15 minutes
- **Workout Only**: 10-15 minutes
- **Full Program**: 20-25 minutes
- **Athlete Performance**: 25-30 minutes
- **Youth Program**: 15-20 minutes
- **General Wellness**: 10-15 minutes
- **Movement Needs**: 15-20 minutes

### Can I Save and Continue Later?

Yes! Your progress is automatically saved as you complete each section. You can close your browser and return anytime to pick up where you left off.

## Completing Your Intake

### Step 1: Choose Your Path

When you first access the intake form, you'll see seven different path options:

#### 1. **Nutrition Only**
Choose this if you want personalized nutrition guidance without a workout program.

**You'll receive:**
- Daily calorie targets
- Macronutrient breakdown
- Meal timing recommendations
- Sample meal plans
- Shopping lists
- Practical nutrition tips

#### 2. **Workout/Training Program Only**
Choose this if you want a personalized workout program without nutrition planning.

**You'll receive:**
- Custom training schedule
- Exercise selection based on your equipment and experience
- Sets, reps, and rest periods
- Progression plan
- Exercise instructions and safety notes

#### 3. **Full Program**
Choose this if you want both nutrition and workout guidance for comprehensive support.

**You'll receive:**
- Everything from Nutrition Only
- Everything from Workout Only

#### 4. **Athlete Performance Program**
Choose this if you're a competitive athlete seeking sport-specific, performance-grade programming based on NSCA-CSCS standards.

**You'll receive:**
- Periodized training blocks
- Power development protocols
- Speed and agility work
- Strength programming
- Sport-specific conditioning
- Recovery strategies
- Optional nutrition guidance

#### 5. **Youth Program**
Choose this if you're a young athlete (or parent of one) seeking age-appropriate, safety-focused guidance.

**You'll receive:**
- Age-appropriate exercises
- Safety-first approach
- Progressive development plan
- Parent/guardian guidance
- Fun, engaging format

#### 6. **General Wellness Guidance**
Choose this if you're seeking general health improvement without athletic complexity.

**You'll receive:**
- Practical wellness guidance
- Simple, sustainable habits
- Basic fitness recommendations
- Nutrition fundamentals
- Lifestyle tips

#### 7. **Movement Needs**
Choose this if you're recovering from injury or managing a chronic condition.

**You'll receive:**
- Safe movement patterns
- Exercises to avoid
- Progressive loading strategies
- Pain management guidance
- Return-to-activity protocols
- Clear safety warnings

### Step 2: Answer the Questions

After selecting your path, you'll be guided through a series of questions tailored to your needs.

#### Tips for Completing the Intake:

**Be Honest and Thorough**
- Provide accurate information about your health, injuries, and limitations
- The more detail you provide, the better we can personalize your plan

**Use the Help Icons**
- Hover over the (?) icons for explanations and examples
- These provide context for questions that might be unclear

**Watch the Progress Bar**
- The progress indicator at the top shows how far you've come
- You can see which sections are complete and which remain

**Answer Required Questions**
- Questions marked with an asterisk (*) are required
- You won't be able to proceed without answering these

**Skip Optional Questions**
- Some questions are optional and can be skipped
- However, more information helps us create better plans

#### Smart Branching

The form adapts to your answers! You'll only see questions relevant to your situation:

- If you say you have no equipment, you won't see equipment-specific questions
- If you indicate an injury, you'll see follow-up questions about it
- If you select certain dietary restrictions, you'll see related preference questions

### Step 3: Review and Submit

Before submitting:

1. **Review Your Answers**: Use the progress tracker to navigate back to any section
2. **Make Changes**: Edit any answers that need updating
3. **Check Required Fields**: Ensure all required questions are answered
4. **Submit**: Click the "Submit Intake" button

### What Happens After Submission?

1. **Confirmation**: You'll see a confirmation message
2. **Processing**: Your packets will be generated (usually within 1-2 minutes)
3. **Notification**: You'll receive an email when your packets are ready
4. **Dashboard Access**: Your packets will appear in your dashboard

## Viewing Your Packets

### Accessing Your Dashboard

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to your Dashboard
3. You'll see all your packets listed

### Understanding Packet Status

**Pending** (⏳)
- Your packet is being generated
- This usually takes 1-2 minutes
- Refresh the page to check for updates

**Ready** (✓)
- Your packet is complete and ready to view
- Click "View" to see the content
- Click "Download PDF" to save a copy

**Failed** (✗)
- Something went wrong during generation
- Our team has been notified
- We'll regenerate it and notify you when it's ready

### Viewing Packet Content

When you open a packet, you'll see:

1. **Overview Section**: Summary of your plan
2. **Detailed Sections**: Organized content specific to your packet type
3. **Action Items**: Clear next steps and recommendations
4. **Resources**: Additional tips, links, or references

### Downloading Packets

**To Download as PDF:**
1. Open the packet you want to download
2. Click the "Download PDF" button
3. The PDF will save to your device
4. You can print it or save it for offline access

**To Print:**
1. Open the packet
2. Click the "Print" button
3. Use your browser's print dialog
4. Select your printer or "Save as PDF"

### Multiple Packets

If you selected a path that includes multiple packets (like Full Program), you'll see:

- **Nutrition Packet**: Your personalized nutrition plan
- **Workout Packet**: Your personalized training program

Each packet can be viewed and downloaded independently.

## Shopping for Merchandise

### Browsing the Shop

Visit the **Shop** page to browse AFYA merchandise. The shop features:

- **Apparel**: T-shirts, hoodies, shorts, and more
- **Accessories**: Water bottles, gym bags, resistance bands, and other fitness gear
- **Drops**: Limited-time exclusive items with countdown timers
- **Collections**: Curated product bundles and starter packs

### Filtering Products

Use the category tabs at the top of the shop page to filter products:

- **All**: View all available products
- **Apparel**: Clothing items only
- **Accessories**: Non-clothing items only
- **Drops**: Limited-time exclusive items only

### Viewing Product Details

Click on any product to see:

- High-quality product images (multiple angles)
- Detailed description
- Price
- Available sizes and colors
- Size guide
- Care instructions
- Customer reviews (if available)

### Adding to Cart

1. Select your size (if applicable)
2. Select your color (if applicable)
3. Click "Add to Cart"
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### Checkout Process

**Step 1: Review Your Cart**
- View all items in your cart
- Adjust quantities or remove items
- See subtotal, tax, and shipping estimates
- Click "Proceed to Checkout"

**Step 2: Choose Donation Allocation**

Every purchase includes a 25% donation to support AFYA's community programs. Choose where your donation goes:

- **Foundations**: Supports AFYA's general operations and community programs
- **Sponsor-A-Client**: Directly funds wellness packets for clients in need

This is a required step - you must select an allocation to continue.

**Step 3: Enter Shipping Information**
- Full name
- Shipping address
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)

**Step 4: Payment**

We accept:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Apple Pay (on Safari/iOS devices)
- Google Pay (on Chrome/Android devices)
- Link by Stripe (one-click checkout for returning customers)

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We never store your payment information.

**Step 5: Confirmation**

After successful payment:
- You'll see an order confirmation page
- You'll receive a confirmation email with order details
- You'll receive tracking information when your order ships

### Order Tracking

**To Track Your Order:**
1. Check your confirmation email for the tracking link
2. Or log in to your account and view order history
3. Click on your order to see current status

**Order Status:**
- **Pending**: Payment is being processed
- **Processing**: Your order is being prepared
- **Shipped**: Your order is on its way (tracking number provided)
- **Delivered**: Your order has been delivered

### Returns and Refunds

**Return Policy:**
- 30-day return window from delivery date
- Items must be unworn and in original condition with tags
- Customer pays return shipping (unless item is defective)

**To Request a Return:**
1. Contact support@afyaperformance.com within 30 days
2. Include your order number and reason for return
3. We'll provide return instructions
4. Ship the item back to us
5. Refund issued within 5-10 business days after we receive the item

## Participating in Impact Programs

### Impact Page Overview

Visit the **Impact** page to learn about and participate in AFYA's community initiatives. The page features:

- **Community Minutes Moved Counter**: See the total minutes of movement logged by all AFYA clients
- **Four Impact Programs**: Donations, Sponsor-A-Client, Gear Drive, and Equipment Donation (Coming Soon)

### Community Minutes Moved

The prominent counter on the Impact page shows the collective impact of the AFYA community. Every time a client logs activity in their dashboard, it contributes to this total, demonstrating the power of movement across our entire community.

### Making a Donation

**Why Donate?**
- Support AFYA's operations and community programs
- Fund wellness packets for clients in need
- Enable program expansion and development

**How to Donate:**
1. Visit the Impact page
2. Click "Donate Now" in the Donations section
3. Choose donation amount (one-time or recurring)
4. Select allocation (Foundations or Sponsor-A-Client)
5. Enter donor information
6. Complete payment through Stripe
7. Receive donation receipt via email

**Tax Deductibility:**
- AFYA is a registered nonprofit (if applicable)
- You'll receive a tax receipt for your donation
- Consult your tax advisor for deduction eligibility

### Sponsor-A-Client Program

**What is Sponsor-A-Client?**

The Sponsor-A-Client program allows community members to fund personalized wellness packets for clients who cannot afford them. Your sponsorship directly helps someone access the guidance they need to improve their health and fitness.

**How It Works:**
1. You make a donation designated for Sponsor-A-Client
2. AFYA matches your donation with a client in need
3. The client receives their personalized packet(s) at no cost
4. You receive updates on the impact of your sponsorship (anonymized for privacy)

**To Become a Sponsor:**
1. Visit the Impact page
2. Click "Become a Sponsor" in the Sponsor-A-Client section
3. Choose sponsorship level or custom amount
4. Complete donation process
5. Receive sponsorship confirmation and impact updates

### Gear Drive (Active Program)

**What is the Gear Drive?**

The Gear Drive accepts donations of used workout clothing for:
- **Recycling**: Items too worn for reuse are sent to textile recycling facilities
- **Upcycling**: Items are repaired or repurposed creatively
- **Redistribution**: Good condition items are donated to clients in need
- **Community Events**: Items support youth sports programs and community fitness events

**What We Accept:**
- Athletic shirts and tank tops
- Workout shorts and leggings
- Athletic shoes (gently used)
- Sports bras
- Gym socks
- Sweatshirts and hoodies
- Athletic jackets

**Condition Guidelines:**
- **Excellent**: Like new, minimal wear
- **Good**: Gently used, no major flaws
- **Fair**: Used but functional, minor flaws acceptable
- **Worn**: Heavily used but still intact (recycling only)

**How to Donate:**

1. Visit the Impact page
2. Click "Donate Gear" in the Gear Drive section
3. Fill out the donation form:
   - Your contact information
   - Types of items you're donating
   - Estimated quantity
   - Condition assessment
   - Preferred dropoff method
4. Choose dropoff method:
   - **Dropoff**: Bring items to our location
   - **Pickup**: We'll pick up from your address (for larger donations)
   - **Shipping**: Ship items to us (we'll provide address)
5. Submit the form
6. Receive confirmation email with next steps

**After Submission:**
- You'll receive a confirmation number
- We'll contact you within 24 hours to coordinate logistics
- You'll receive a reminder before your scheduled dropoff/pickup
- After we receive your items, you'll get a thank you email with impact information

### Equipment Donation (Coming Soon)

This program will accept donations of workout equipment (dumbbells, kettlebells, resistance bands, etc.) in the future. Check back for updates!

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I know which path to choose?**

A: Read the descriptions carefully. If you're unsure:
- Choose "Full Program" for comprehensive support
- Choose "General Wellness" if you're just starting out
- Choose specific paths (Nutrition Only, Workout Only) if you only need one type of guidance

**Q: Can I change my path after starting?**

A: Yes! Use the "Back" button to return to path selection before submitting. After submission, you'll need to contact support to retake the intake.

**Q: What if I don't know the answer to a question?**

A: Provide your best estimate. For optional questions, you can skip them. For required questions, choose the closest option or use the "Other" field if available.

**Q: Is my information secure?**

A: Yes! All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We follow industry best practices for data protection. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Intake Questions

**Q: How long is my saved progress kept?**

A: Your progress is saved indefinitely until you submit or delete it. You can return anytime to complete your intake.

**Q: Can I edit my answers after submitting?**

A: Not directly. If you need to update your information significantly, contact support to retake the intake. For minor updates, your coach can adjust your packets.

**Q: What if I accidentally close the browser?**

A: No problem! Your progress is automatically saved. Just log back in and continue where you left off.

**Q: Why am I seeing different questions than my friend?**

A: The intake adapts to your answers. You'll only see questions relevant to your path and previous responses.

### Packet Questions

**Q: When will my packets be ready?**

A: Most packets are generated within 1-2 minutes. Complex packets (like Athlete Performance) may take up to 5 minutes. You'll receive an email notification when they're ready.

**Q: What if my packet shows "Failed"?**

A: Don't worry! Our team is automatically notified and will regenerate your packet. You'll receive an email when it's ready. This is rare and usually resolves quickly.

**Q: Can I request changes to my packet?**

A: Yes! Contact your coach or support team with specific requests. They can edit your packet or regenerate it with adjustments.

**Q: How often should I update my intake?**

A: We recommend updating your intake:
- Every 3-6 months for general progress
- When your goals change significantly
- After major life changes (injury, schedule changes, etc.)
- When you reach a plateau

**Q: Can I share my packet with others?**

A: Your packets are personalized for you. While you can share the PDF, the recommendations are specific to your profile and may not be appropriate for others.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**

A: We support:
- Chrome (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Edge

For the best experience, use the latest version of your browser.

**Q: Can I complete the intake on my phone?**

A: Yes! The intake form is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. However, viewing packets is easier on a tablet or desktop.

**Q: What if I encounter an error?**

A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support if the issue persists

**Q: Why isn't my progress saving?**

A: Ensure:
- You're logged in
- Your internet connection is stable
- Cookies are enabled in your browser
- You're not in private/incognito mode

### Shop Questions

**Q: How long does shipping take?**

A: Orders typically ship within 2-3 business days. Delivery time depends on your location:
- Standard shipping: 5-7 business days
- Express shipping: 2-3 business days (if available)

**Q: What if my item doesn't fit?**

A: We accept returns within 30 days. Contact support@afyaperformance.com to initiate a return. You'll be responsible for return shipping unless the item is defective.

**Q: Can I change my order after placing it?**

A: If your order hasn't shipped yet, contact us immediately and we'll try to accommodate changes. Once shipped, you'll need to return the item for a refund.

**Q: What is the donation allocation?**

A: 25% of every purchase goes to support AFYA's community programs. You choose whether it goes to Foundations (general operations) or Sponsor-A-Client (funding packets for those in need).

**Q: Do you ship internationally?**

A: Currently, we only ship within the United States. International shipping may be available in the future.

**Q: What are "Drops"?**

A: Drops are limited-time exclusive items available for a short period. They often feature special designs or collaborations. When they're gone, they're gone!

**Q: How do I know my size?**

A: Each product page includes a size guide. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

### Impact Program Questions

**Q: Is my donation tax-deductible?**

A: If AFYA is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit, your donation may be tax-deductible. You'll receive a receipt for tax purposes. Consult your tax advisor for specific guidance.

**Q: Can I choose which client my sponsorship supports?**

A: For privacy reasons, we don't match specific sponsors with specific clients. However, you'll receive anonymized updates about the impact of your sponsorship.

**Q: What happens to gear that's too worn to redistribute?**

A: Items that are too worn for reuse are sent to textile recycling facilities, preventing them from ending up in landfills. This is an environmentally responsible way to dispose of old athletic wear.

**Q: Can I get a receipt for my gear donation?**

A: Yes! After we receive and process your donation, we'll send you a receipt documenting the items and estimated value for tax purposes.

**Q: How do I know my gear donation made an impact?**

A: We send follow-up emails with impact statistics, including how many items were recycled, redistributed, or used for community events.

**Q: Can I donate equipment now?**

A: Not yet! The Equipment Donation program is coming soon. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when it launches.

### Getting Help

**Q: Who can I contact for help?**

A: Reach out to our support team:
- Email: support@afyaperformance.com
- Use the "Contact" link in your dashboard or footer
- Response time: Usually within 24 hours

**Q: Can I schedule a call with a coach?**

A: Yes! Premium members can schedule coaching calls. Check your account settings or contact support to upgrade.

**Q: What if I have a question about my specific plan?**

A: Your coach can answer questions about your personalized plan. Use the messaging feature in your dashboard or email your assigned coach.

**Q: How do I report a problem with the website?**

A: Email support@afyaperformance.com with:
- Description of the problem
- What page you were on
- What browser you're using
- Screenshots if possible

We'll investigate and respond within 24 hours.

### Before Starting Your Intake

- [ ] Set aside uninterrupted time (15-30 minutes depending on path)

- [ ] Read questions carefully

- [ ] Use the help icons if you're unsure about a question
